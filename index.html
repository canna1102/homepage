About Me

My name is YANG, Yi （杨溢）. Currently I am an M.phil. student at Department of Computer Science and Engineering, the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, supervised by Prof.Huamin Qu in VisLab. Before that, I was a member of Mtrec lab focusing on image processing topics. I received my B.Sc. at Electronic Science and Engineering from Nanjing University.

In my spare time, I enjoy various sports like hiking, swimming, badminton and squash. I have been fascinated by photography and post-processing techniques, thus I worked as a photographer intern for a local student newspaper TwoTown in Nanjing, in the last months of my undergraduate life.

During my research on data visualization in VisLab, I experience a complete pipeline of the development for a visual analytics system, from pilot study at the beginning, data processinng such as data collecting and modeling, visual design and interface design, web app development and user experience study at the end. It is really an excited experience and I hope to continue with my interest in data visualization after my graduation at Jan.2016.

Please contact me if you find my profile interesting : )
