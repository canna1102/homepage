Research Project

Abstract

In this demo, we develop a system to simulate thousands of taxis simultaneously in Shenzhen.

With on the system, we discover some hiring patterns in the temporal domain. For example, a morning peak is identified. First, the overall hiring ratio is low in the early morning around 5:00AM (less than 15%). The hiring ratio starts to increase at 6:00AM. Then, a local peak is reached at 8:00AM to 9:00AM (around 50%).
